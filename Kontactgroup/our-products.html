About Kontact

Kontact Petro-Chem Pvt. Ltd. is one of the most leading company for supplying of various Raw Materials for Paints & Inks like Mineral Turpentine Oil (BPCL) & Driers, Pigments, Acrylic Emulsions, Additives & many more different products since 1985.

We also manufactures all kind of Metal Tins & M.S. Drums in our sister concern firm Kontact Metal Cans Located in Santej, Gandhinagar.

We have dedicated unit as Kontact Enterprise to supply Mineral Turpentine Oil (BPCL) to ensure the prompt delivery.
